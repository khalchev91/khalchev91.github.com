Upcoming Technology in Education

Virtual Reality is a major breakthrough in Technology in all industries. One way this would affect the education industry is by giving students the ability to visit anywhere in the world. Through the use of something like Virtual Reality Headset a student can be able to jump to places where a field trip alone would suffice. This would take away the need for a field trip as Virtual Reality pushes the edges of what is possible while staying at one fix position.
