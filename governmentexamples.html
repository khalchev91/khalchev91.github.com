Different methods have been used by various candidates whether by going out and campaigning or by the use of social media. The use of social media is a great tool to the candidate whether against them or for them. They use crowdfunding to make money for their campaign and you have people putting in a lot of money for the campaign. This goes to show the power of social media in the case of a general election. Using social media and online crowdfunding would take reduce the time the candidate has to spend walking around asking for votes and holding press conferences which would reduce the money spent on physicsl campaigns.
